DATA PROCESSING AGREEMENT (DPA)

Between: Funcxon (Pty) Ltd ("Processor/Platform")
And: Vendor/Subscriber ("Responsible Party")

Effective Date: 05 December 2025

This Data Processing Agreement (“Agreement”) forms part of the Funcxon Terms & Conditions
and governs the processing of personal information by Funcxon (Pty) Ltd (“Funcxon”) on behalf
of the Vendor/Subscriber (the “Vendor”) for the purpose of providing platform services under
the Protection of Personal Information Act 4 of 2013 ("POPIA").



1. DEFINITIONS

   •   Personal Information: As defined in POPIA.

   •   Processing: Any operation involving personal information.

   •   Responsible Party: The Vendor who determines the purpose of processing.

   •   Operator: Funcxon (Pty) Ltd acting on behalf of Vendor.

   •   Data Subject: Individual to whom the personal information relates.



2. ROLE OF THE PARTIES

   •   Vendor is the Responsible Party.

   •   Funcxon is the Operator and processes personal information only on documented
       instructions of the Vendor.



3. PURPOSE OF PROCESSING

Funcxon will process personal information solely to:

   •   Host Vendor profile and content

   •   Enable communication with customers

   •   Facilitate bookings and transactions

   •   Provide payment services through third-party providers

   •   Provide platform analytics and improvements

   •   Customer dispute resolution and support

No other use is permitted unless required by law.



4. TYPES OF PERSONAL INFORMATION

Typical information processed includes:
   •   Customer identification details

   •   Contact information

   •   Booking and event details

   •   Payment and invoice information

   •   Communication and review data



5. RESPONSIBILITIES OF THE VENDOR

Vendor represents and warrants that:

   •   Personal information is obtained lawfully

   •   Data subjects are informed of processing activities

   •   Necessary consents are obtained where required

   •   Vendor complies with POPIA obligations

Vendor indemnifies Funcxon for any breach of POPIA by the Vendor.



6. OBLIGATIONS OF FUNCXON (OPERATOR)

Funcxon will:

   •   Process only on documented instructions from Vendor

   •   Implement appropriate security safeguards

   •   Restrict internal access on a need-to-know basis

   •   Notify Vendor of any data breach without undue delay

   •   Cooperate with Vendor in responding to data subject requests

   •   Not engage another operator without safeguards in place



7. SUB-PROCESSORS

Funcxon may use third-party providers to deliver services, including:

   •   Cloud hosting providers

   •   Payment gateways

   •   Analytics providers

Funcxon remains responsible for ensuring sub-processors meet POPIA requirements.



8. INTERNATIONAL DATA TRANSFERS
Where personal information is transferred outside South Africa, Funcxon will ensure legal
safeguards exist including:

   •   Adequate data protection laws, or

   •   Standard contractual clauses, or

   •   Written undertakings compatible with POPIA.



9. SECURITY SAFEGUARDS

Funcxon will maintain:

   •   Encryption where appropriate

   •   Secure access control

   •   Firewalls and intrusion detection

   •   Regular vulnerability assessments

   •   Backup and disaster recovery procedures



10. DATA BREACH NOTIFICATION

In the event of a confirmed data breach, Funcxon will:

   •   Notify Vendor promptly

   •   Provide details of the breach

   •   Assist with mitigation efforts

   •   Cooperate with regulatory reporting duties

Vendor is responsible for notification to data subjects unless agreed otherwise.



11. DATA SUBJECT RIGHTS

Funcxon will assist Vendor, where reasonable, with:

   •   Access to personal information

   •   Rectification or deletion requests

   •   Objections to processing

   •   Complaints or enquiries from data subjects



12. AUDITS

Vendor may request documentation demonstrating compliance. On-site audits are permitted
only:
   •   With reasonable notice, and

   •   Not more than once annually, unless mandated by law



13. RETENTION AND DELETION

Upon termination of the Vendor’s contract:

   •   Funcxon will delete or anonymise personal information after legal retention periods

   •   Backups will be purged during routine cycles

Exception: Information required for legal claims or compliance may be retained.



14. CONFIDENTIALITY

Funcxon must ensure that authorised personnel:

   •   Maintain strict confidentiality, and

   •   Receive training regarding POPIA compliance



15. LIABILITY

Vendor is responsible for:

   •   Lawful data collection

   •   Accuracy of information supplied

   •   Consent obligations

Funcxon is liable for breaches caused by its own negligence or security failure.



16. TERM AND TERMINATION

This Agreement remains in force for the duration of the Vendor’s subscription to Funcxon
services and will automatically terminate upon full deletion of Vendor data.



17. GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement is governed by South African law.
