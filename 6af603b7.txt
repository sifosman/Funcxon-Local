1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. Cookies
allow the website to recognise your device and store certain information about your preferences
and interactions.

Cookies may be:

   •   Session Cookies: Deleted when you close your browser.

   •   Persistent Cookies: Remain stored until they expire or are deleted.
